Home-made Pasta

Description

Mama's Banana Pudding is a classic American dessert that combines creamy pudding, sweet bananas, and crunchy cookies. The recipe, passed down through generations, is a masterclass in comfort food.

Ingredients

  • cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 6 cups whole milk
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 9 ripe bananas
  • 2 (12 ounce) boxes vanilla wafers
  • 6 egg whites (see “Test Kitchen Tip”)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
